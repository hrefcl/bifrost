# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}_

## 1. Scope

This Policy describes how the organization operating this platform (the "Organization") processes
personal information in the context of the communication and collaboration Service. The Organization
is the data controller; the technical operator acts as a processor according to its instructions.

## 2. Information processed

Depending on the components enabled, the Service may process:

- **Account and identity data:** name, email address, credentials, preferences.
- **User content:** email messages, calendar events, contacts, tasks, files, meeting notes and other
  content you create or receive.
- **Operational and technical data:** access logs, IP addresses, device and browser identifiers,
  timestamps, security and diagnostic events.
- **Usage data and metrics:** aggregated utilization statistics for reporting, capacity and Service
  improvement.

## 3. Purposes of processing

Information is processed for legitimate purposes, including: provision and operation of the Service,
authentication and access control, security and abuse prevention, support, operational continuity,
metrics and reporting, automation, business intelligence and compliance with applicable legal and
regulatory obligations.

## 4. Lawful basis

Processing relies, as applicable, on the performance of the service relationship, the Organization's
legitimate interest in operating and securing the platform, consent where required, and compliance
with legal obligations.

## 5. Retention

Information is retained for as long as necessary for the described purposes and in accordance with
the Organization's Data Retention Policy and applicable law. Evidence of policy acceptance is
retained as a legal record for as long as its evidentiary value persists.

## 6. Processors and third parties

The Organization may rely on infrastructure and service providers (for example, hosting, storage or
mail delivery) acting as processors under agreements that require adequate levels of security and
confidentiality. Personal information is not sold.

## 7. Transfers

Where data transfers to other jurisdictions occur, the Organization will seek to ensure they are
carried out with adequate safeguards under applicable law.

## 8. Individual rights

Subject to applicable law, you may exercise rights of access, rectification, erasure, objection,
restriction and portability regarding your personal data by contacting the Organization through the
channels it provides. The Organization will respond within the legal timeframes.

## 9. Security

The Organization applies reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information
against unauthorized access, loss or alteration. See the Security Policy.

## 10. Changes

This Policy may be updated. When a mandatory version changes, your acceptance will be requested
before continuing. The date of the last update is shown at the top.

## 11. Contact

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights, contact the Organization through its support
channels or its data protection officer, if any.
