# Audit Policy

_Last updated: {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}_

## 1. Purpose

This Policy describes how the Organization logs and reviews relevant Service activity for security,
integrity, compliance and operational continuity purposes.

## 2. What is logged

Depending on configuration, events such as the following may be logged: sign-ins and authentication,
configuration and permission changes, administrative access, operations on sensitive data, security
events, policy acceptance and system errors. Logs include technical context (timestamp, actor
identifier, IP address and action type).

## 3. Purpose of logging

Audit logs allow incidents to be detected and diagnosed, compliance to be demonstrated, misuse to be
investigated and security to be improved. They are not used for unjustified monitoring of individual
performance beyond what is necessary for those purposes.

## 4. Integrity and access

Logs are protected against alteration and access is restricted to authorized personnel. Policy
acceptance evidence is retained as an append-only record with integrity verification.

## 5. Retention

Logs are retained for a defined period in accordance with the Data Retention Policy and applicable
law, after which they are securely deleted or anonymized.

## 6. Transparency

Subject to applicable law, users may request information about the processing of their data in the
logs, subject to the corresponding security safeguards.

## 7. Changes

This Policy may be updated. The date of the last update is shown at the top.
