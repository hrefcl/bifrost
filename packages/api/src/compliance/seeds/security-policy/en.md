# Security Policy

_Last updated: {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}_

## 1. Purpose

This Policy describes the security principles the Organization applies to protect information and the
communication and collaboration services.

## 2. Access control

Access is based on per-user authentication and the principle of least privilege. Administrative roles
are granted restrictively and reviewed periodically. Strong authentication is recommended and, where
available, required.

## 3. Data protection

Information in transit is protected with encryption on channels that support it. Sensitive
information at rest is protected according to the capabilities of the Organization's infrastructure.

## 4. User responsibilities

Users must protect their credentials, not share accounts, report incidents and follow the Acceptable
Use Policy. Security is a shared responsibility.

## 5. Incident management

The Organization maintains processes to detect, contain, investigate and report security incidents in
accordance with applicable law, and notifies affected parties where appropriate.

## 6. Continuity

The Organization strives to maintain Service availability through backup, monitoring and recovery
practices appropriate to its operational context.

## 7. Continuous improvement

Security controls are reviewed and updated periodically to respond to new threats.

## 8. Changes

This Policy may be updated. The date of the last update is shown at the top.
