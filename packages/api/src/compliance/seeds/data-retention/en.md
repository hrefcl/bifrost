# Data Retention Policy

_Last updated: {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}_

## 1. Purpose

This Policy describes how the Organization retains and deletes information processed through the
Service, balancing operational and legal needs with data minimization.

## 2. Principles

- Information is retained only for as long as necessary for its purposes.
- Retention periods are defined by data type and service category.
- Once the applicable period has elapsed, information is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 3. Categories and criteria (indicative)

- **User content** (email, calendar, contacts, files): retained while the account is active and
  according to the Organization's rules; may be deleted after account closure.
- **Operational and security logs:** retained for a bounded period for diagnostics, security and
  compliance.
- **Aggregated metrics:** may be retained in anonymized form for extended periods.
- **Policy acceptance evidence:** retained as a legal record for as long as its evidentiary value
  persists, even after account closure, anonymizing personal identifiers where appropriate.

## 4. Deletion and right to erasure

Where applicable law recognizes the right to erasure, the Organization will handle requests within
the legal timeframes, without prejudice to retention obligations imposed by law itself.

## 5. Backups

Information present in backups is removed according to the rotation cycle of those backups, which may
differ from deletion in primary systems.

## 6. Changes

This Policy may be updated. The date of the last update is shown at the top.
