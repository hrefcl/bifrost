# Acceptable Use Policy

_Last updated: {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}_

## 1. Purpose

This Policy defines acceptable use of the communication and collaboration Service in order to protect
users, the Organization and the integrity of the systems.

## 2. Permitted use

The Service must be used for legitimate purposes related to the Organization's activity and in
compliance with applicable law, the Terms of Service and internal policies.

## 3. Prohibited conduct

It is prohibited, among other things, to use the Service to:

- send unsolicited bulk email (spam), phishing or identity spoofing;
- distribute malware, harmful code or unlawful content;
- gain unauthorized access to accounts, data or systems, or attempt to bypass security controls;
- interfere with the availability or performance of the Service (for example, denial-of-service
  attacks or abusive resource consumption);
- infringe the intellectual property or privacy rights of third parties;
- collect or process personal data without a legitimate basis;
- circumvent usage limits, quotas or control mechanisms established by the Organization.

## 4. Account security

Users must protect their credentials, use strong authentication where available, and promptly report
any incident or unauthorized use.

## 5. Resources and quotas

The use of storage, mail sending and other resources may be subject to limits and quotas defined by
the Organization to preserve the stability and deliverability of the Service.

## 6. Monitoring

For security, operational continuity and compliance purposes, the Organization may log and analyze
technical and usage events, in accordance with the Privacy Policy and the Audit Policy.

## 7. Consequences of non-compliance

Failure to comply with this Policy may result in the restriction or suspension of access, in addition
to any applicable legal action.

## 8. Changes

This Policy may be updated. When a mandatory version changes, your acceptance will be requested
before continuing. The date of the last update is shown at the top.
